About

My Story

My name is Noah Webster. I am a UX Designer based out of Salt Lake City. Although I have spent most of my life involved with art, designing, or creativity, I am a newcomer to the world of design.

I have spent most of my career in the blue collar world. By working in a high end finish carpentry wood shop, I learned extreme attention to detail. By working as a department head at a clothing fulfillment warehouse, I learned the value of organization and good team collaboration.

The CareerFoundry course was instrumental in my career change. They have allowed me to learn and master tools such as Adobe XD, Figma, Sketch, Keynote, and UsabilityHub. I have also learned the value of wire framing, user testing, prototyping, and user personas. I have a strong passion for creativity and design, and I am excited to continue designing for users.
